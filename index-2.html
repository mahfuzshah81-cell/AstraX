Can I customize AstraX templates to fit my brand?

Yes! AstraX templates are fully customizable, allowing you to tailor colors, fonts, layouts, and other design elements to align with your brand identity. Whether you need minor adjustments or a complete overhaul, you can easily modify the templates to suit your unique vision.

What kind of support does AstraX offer?

AstraX provides comprehensive support, including:

  2. Email Support Reach out to our team for assistance with technical issues or customization queries.
  3. Ticket System Submit support tickets for prioritized assistance from our team: https://alithemes.ticksy.com/

Do AstraX templates work with CMS platforms like WordPress?

No, AstraX templates are built with NextJS 14 and React 18 and do not natively integrate with CMS platforms like WordPress. However, developers can manually convert them into WordPress themes or integrate them into other CMS platforms if needed.

Can I request a custom template design?

Yes! AstraX welcomes template design requests. If you have specific requirements or a unique concept in mind, you can submit a request, and our team will review it for future template development. While we cannot guarantee every request will be fulfilled, we always consider user feedback when creating new designs.

What are the main differences between the Regular License and the Extended License?

In simple terms, the main difference is that under the Regular License, your end product (incorporating the item you've licensed) is distributed for free, whereas under the Extended License your end product may be sold.
